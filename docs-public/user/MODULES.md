# MODULES.md

## Purpose

This document explains the main modules of Super Mechanic.

It is intended for:
- workshop staff
- managers
- administrative users

Goal:
Understand what each part of the system does and when to use it.

---

## System Overview

Super Mechanic is divided into modules.

Each module represents a key part of workshop operations.

---

## 1. Clients

### What it is
A registry of all customers.

### What you can do
- create clients
- edit client information
- view client history

### Why it matters
Every operation is linked to a client.

---

## 2. Vehicles

### What it is
A registry of all vehicles.

### What you can do
- register vehicles
- link vehicles to clients
- track vehicle history

### Why it matters
All work is performed on vehicles.

---

## 3. Processes

### What it is
A workflow for each vehicle.

### Examples
- maintenance
- delivery preparation
- paperwork

### What you can do
- create a process
- move through steps
- track progress

### Why it matters
This is the core of daily operations.

---

## 4. CRM

### What it is
A system for managing relationships and follow-ups.

### Includes
- pipeline
- tasks
- alerts

### What you can do
- track opportunities
- assign tasks
- follow up with clients

### Why it matters
Keeps communication and sales organized.

---

## 5. Tasks

### What it is
Individual actions that need to be completed.

### What you can do
- create tasks
- assign tasks
- mark tasks as completed

### Why it matters
Tasks represent daily work.

---

## 6. Alerts

### What it is
System-generated signals.

### Examples
- overdue tasks
- delayed processes
- follow-up needed

### Important
Alerts are automatic.  
You do not create them manually.

### Why it matters
They show what needs attention.

---

## 7. Appointments

### What it is
Scheduled activities.

### What you can do
- create appointments
- assign time slots
- track daily schedule

### Why it matters
Organizes time and workload.

---

## 8. Quotes

### What it is
Price estimates for work.

### What you can do
- create quotes
- send to clients
- convert into invoices

---

## 9. Invoices

### What it is
Final billing for services.

### What you can do
- generate invoices
- track payments

---

## 10. Dashboard ("Mi trabajo")

### What it is
Your daily control center.

### What it shows
- tasks
- alerts
- processes
- appointments

Grouped by priority:
- Critical
- Attention
- Normal

### Why it matters
This is where you start your day.

---

## How Modules Work Together

Example:

1. Create client
2. Register vehicle
3. Create process
4. Add tasks
5. System generates alerts
6. Schedule appointments
7. Complete process
8. Generate invoice

---

## Key Principle

Everything in the system is connected.

- clients → vehicles
- vehicles → processes
- processes → tasks
- tasks → alerts
- appointments → scheduling

---

## Final Note

Do not treat modules as separate tools.

👉 They work together as one system.
